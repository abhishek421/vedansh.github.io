ABOUT US

Vedansh Infra Services

When our founder, Mr. Lokendra Upadhyay, established Vedansh in 2006, his goal was to raise the bar in the electrical engineering and contracting industry by providing quality workmanship. Started as a plant erection and manpower sourcing company with a team of 5 multi-talented people, today Vedansh is a family of 500 and an industry leader in Electrical and Instrumentation Services, and EPC (Engineering, Procurement and Commissioning).

The Vedansh Touch

At the heart of this exponential growth has been a commitment to safety and quality combined with the immense knowledge and experience of the team to serve you in the best way possible. Our customer-centric approach and passion for work shows in our before-time completion of projects, and 0% casualty rates. Our projects undergo rigorous safety checks before handover, ensuring durability and efficiency to ensure your satisfaction.

Our Promise

Since more than a decade, Vedansh has been the symbol of quality and safety with our commitment to your satisfaction being at the forefront of any endeavour. We go the extra mile to give you the best experience by fulfilling their vision with our planning and execution expertise. We offer turn-key solutions with our complete range of Engineering Design, Procurement and Commissioning, Project Management, Plant Erection, Testing and Commissioning and Operation and Maintenance Services.

We Reach the Peak

When you engage with Vedansh for your project, you can trust us on every step of the way. Right from Project Allocation all the way to Project Review and Handover, the entire process has been subdivided with milestones and checkpoints throughout, to ensure smooth and safe workflow and the assurance of quality that comes with Vedansh.
